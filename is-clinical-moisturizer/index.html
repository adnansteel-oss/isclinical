Hyaluronic Acid

What It Does

Hyaluronic Acid is a powerful humectant naturally found in the skin, capable of holding up to 1,000 times its weight in water. In an iS Clinical moisturizer, it draws moisture from the environment into the skin, providing deep and lasting hydration.

This action helps to visibly plump the skin, smoothing out fine lines and wrinkles for a more youthful appearance. Furthermore, it supports the skin's natural ability to retain moisture, ensuring a consistently hydrated and supple feel.

Expert Note:

While often associated with surface hydration, fragmented or low molecular weight hyaluronic acid can penetrate deeper, offering more profound and sustained moisturization benefits.

Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid)

What It Does

Vitamin C, particularly in its stable L-Ascorbic Acid form, is a potent antioxidant crucial for skin health and vitality. It actively protects the skin from environmental damage caused by free radicals, which can lead to premature aging.

Beyond its protective qualities, Vitamin C also plays a vital role in stimulating collagen synthesis, which improves skin firmness and reduces the appearance of wrinkles.
