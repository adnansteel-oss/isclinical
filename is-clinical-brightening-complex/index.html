What It Does

Alpha-Arbutin is a key player in the complex's brightening power, renowned for its ability to safely and effectively lighten dark spots and hyperpigmentation. It works by inhibiting tyrosinase, the enzyme responsible for melanin production in the skin, without causing cytotoxicity to melanocytes.
