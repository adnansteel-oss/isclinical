1. Salix Alba (Willow) Bark Extract (Source of Salicylic Acid)

Willow bark extract is a gentle, natural resurfacing ingredient. As a oil-soluble beta-hydroxy acid, it penetrates deep into pores to dissolve sebum and debris, helping to minimize breakouts and smooth uneven skin texture.

2. Sugarcane Extract (Source of Glycolic Acid)

Sugarcane extract is an alpha-hydroxy acid that works on the surface of the skin. It helps break the bonds holding dead skin cells together, promoting cellular turnover and revealing a brighter, softer complexion.

3. Centella Asiatica (Asiaticoside, Asiatic Acid, and Madecassic Acid)

Also known as Cica or Gotu Kola, Centella Asiatica is a highly effective antioxidant. It helps soothe hypersensitive skin, supports collagen synthesis, and aids in repairing damaged skin barriers.

4. Chamomile and Green Tea Extracts

These botanical extracts provide additional antioxidant protection. They shield the skin from environmental stressors while calming irritation and reducing redness.

How to Use iS Clinical Cleansing Complex

To get the best results from your gentle exfoliating face wash, incorporate it into your morning and evening skincare routines using these steps:
Dampen Skin: Splash your face and neck with lukewarm water.
Apply a Small Amount: Squeeze a dime-sized amount of the gel cleanser into the palm of your hand.
Emulsify and Massage: Rub your hands together to create a light, soft lather. Gently massage the cleanser over your face and neck in upward, circular motions for about 30 to 60 seconds.
Rinse Thoroughly: Rinse with lukewarm water and pat dry with a clean towel.
Safety Warning: Avoid direct contact with the eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
Tip: Because of its soothing properties and low-foaming gel texture, this cleanser also serves as an excellent, non-irritating shaving gel.
